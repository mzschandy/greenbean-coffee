The Beginning

Some time ago, a team of three dreamers got together and tried to find some common ground amongst them all. What they found was a mutual love for coffee. They were also well aware that the planet is dying and were committed to creating something that could be sustainable and environmentall friendly. By combining those two ideas, they gave birth to Greenbean Coffeehouse.

Building a Purpose

Here at Greenbean Coffee we are equally focused on our coffee and the environment - so much so we made it our name!

Our goal is to reduce our carbon footprint by maintaining forward thinking methods

As stewards of the earth, we take responsibility as coffeehouse owners by:

  • Sourcing sustainable beans
  • Packaging eco-friendly waste
  • Managing our waste

    • Pursuing a Goal

    We want our space to be multi-functional and serve as a place to study, hang out, having a meeting, etc. We are open to all.

    We hope our efforts to reduce our carbon footprint will stick with our customers and push for their own individual efforts outside of their space
